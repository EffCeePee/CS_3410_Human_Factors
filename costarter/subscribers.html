Becoming a subscriber is an advanced concept of building and income while also helping those seeking to earn a living.

  • 1

    Join:

    You join CoStarter creating a Subscriber Account to fund projects.

  • 2

    Build up funds:

    You along with other members make regular deposits into a Subscriber Account until that account is sufficient to fund a project.

  • 3

    Select Projects:

    Review and select projects you are interested in funding.

  • 4

    Become an Earning Community Member

    When project is funded you become an Earning Community member monitoring your project and sharing in project revenues.

  • 5

    Collect Returns:

    As profits are generated by your client’s project your share of that profit is returned directly to your account. 6. Do It Again: Continue to build up funds and participate in projects.
